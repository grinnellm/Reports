Biological samples were collected by the \boat{Queens Reach},
a seine test charter vessel funded by DFO.
The primary purpose of the test charter vessel was to
collect biological samples from main bodies of herring from
Haida Gwaii major (priority) and Area 2W minor stock areas,
identified from soundings.

\begin{itemize}

\item The \boat{Queens Reach} operated a 25-day charter
from March \nth{12} to April \nth{5}, collecting samples from HG and Area 2 West.

\item The \boat{Haida Spirit} operated an 18-day dive charter
from April \nth{3} to April \nth{20}.

\item The \boat{Ocean Tigress} operated a spawn reconnaissance charter
for 19 days from March \nth{30} to April \nth{17} with surface survey work.

\end{itemize}
