Biological samples were collected by the \boat{Queens Reach},
a seine test charter vessel funded by DFO:

\begin{itemize}

\item The \boat{Queens Reach} collected biological samples from main bodies of
herring in Haida Gwaii and Area 2 West, identified from soundings, and

\item The \boat{Queens Reach} completed an 8-day surface survey charter in
Area 2 West from April \nth{5} to April \nth{11}.

\end{itemize}

The \boat{Queens Reach} was funded by DFO through a contract to
the Herring  Conservation Research Society.
